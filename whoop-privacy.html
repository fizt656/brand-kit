WLD Health Bridge

Privacy Policy

A private, personal integration for viewing and analyzing the account owner's WHOOP data.

Effective July 19, 2026

Purpose

WLD Health Bridge is a private, single-user integration operated by Gus Halwani. It connects the account owner's WHOOP account to a personally controlled health dashboard for sleep, recovery, strain, and activity tracking.

Data accessed

The integration may access WHOOP sleep, recovery, physiological cycle, workout, and basic account data only after the account owner grants permission through WHOOP OAuth. It does not collect WHOOP credentials or continuous raw heart-rate data.

How data is used

WHOOP data is used only to populate the account owner's private dashboard, reconcile personal tracking records, and generate personal summaries or analyses requested by the account owner. The data is not used for advertising, profiling other people, or automated decisions affecting eligibility, employment, insurance, credit, or medical care.

Storage and sharing

OAuth tokens and synchronized records are stored on systems controlled by the account owner. Health data is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties. Limited infrastructure providers may transmit or host encrypted traffic as necessary to operate the integration.

Control and deletion

The account owner may revoke access at any time from the WHOOP app or account settings. Stored tokens and synchronized WHOOP-derived records can also be deleted on request. Manual corrections in the private dashboard take precedence over imported values.

Security

The integration uses WHOOP's OAuth 2.0 authorization flow, requests only needed scopes, keeps client credentials and tokens out of public source code, and protects its dashboard behind authenticated access.

Contact

Questions about this policy or requests concerning stored data can be submitted through the site contact page.
